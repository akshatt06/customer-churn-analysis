# Customer Churn Analysis using Python

## Project Overview

This project performs Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on a Telco Customer Churn dataset to identify the major drivers influencing customer churn behavior and derive business recommendations to improve customer retention strategies.


## Business Problem

Customer churn represents a critical business challenge in the telecom industry, affecting revenue generation, customer acquisition costs, and long-term profitability.

The objective of this analysis is to understand:

- What factors influence customer churn?
- Which customer groups demonstrate higher churn risk?
- What business actions can improve retention?


## Dataset Overview

The dataset contains customer-level information related to:

- Demographics
- Service subscriptions
- Contract information
- Billing behavior
- Payment methods
- Customer churn status

## Key Business Insights

- Month-to-month contract customers demonstrate significantly higher churn behavior.

- Customers with shorter tenure exhibit increased churn likelihood.

- Higher monthly charges appear associated with elevated churn risk.

- Fiber optic users show comparatively higher churn tendencies.

- Electronic check payment users demonstrate higher customer attrition.


## Strategic Business Recommendations

- Encourage migration toward long-term contracts.

- Improve onboarding and early customer engagement initiatives.

- Review pricing strategy for higher-billing customers.

- Investigate service experience within fiber optic customer segments.

- Increase adoption of automated payment methods.
